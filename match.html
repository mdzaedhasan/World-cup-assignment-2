FIFA World Cup Qatar 2022

A total of 64 games will be played to decide the winners of the FIFA World Cup Qatar 2022™. See when and where the action will take place. The Opening match of the FIFA World Cup 2022™ featuring the Host Country on Monday 21 November 2022 has been reassigned to 17:00 CET / 19:00 Doha Time as part of the match schedule completion after the Final Draw. While the first match of the competition, in practice, will be Senegal vs Netherlands, the Opening Match will be Qatar vs Ecuador played at 17:00 CET / 19:00 Doha Time.
